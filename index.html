H.M. GYANENDRA SHAH

Unity, Sovereignty, and the Sacred Restoration of Nepal

2025: The Tipping Point

Following the September Gen Z Uprising that led to the resignation of the government, public support for the Monarchy has reached historic levels. With general elections now scheduled for March 5, 2026, Nepal stands at its most significant crossroads since 2008.

The Shah Dynasty & The Crown

The Shah dynasty was established by King Prithvi Narayan Shah, the Great Unifier. For over 240 years, the monarchy served as the central symbol of Nepal's unity. His Majesty King Gyanendra remains the living symbol of that sovereign tradition.

"The 2008 transition was an agreement based on the promise of stability. Seventeen years later, it is clear that those promises were broken by the political elite."

The 2025 Restoration Movement

The movement has transitioned from nostalgia to an organized political force through these key events:

  • March 9, 2025: Over 400,000 supporters staged a Grand Welcome at Tribhuvan International Airport.
  • March 27, 2025: Formation of the Joint People’s Movement Committee.
  • September 2025: Youth-led anti-corruption protests highlighted the failure of the republican experiment, positioning the Monarchy as the necessary Guardian of the state.

The Vision for 2026

We advocate for a return to the 1991 Constitution, adapted for the modern era:

  • Constitutional Monarchy: A non-partisan Head of State to provide stability.
  • Hindu Kingdom: Reclaiming Nepal’s spiritual identity while ensuring religious freedom.
  • Economic Integrity: Ending the cycle of 14 governments in 17 years.
